# Privacy and Security Policy — Healthcare Planner

**Last updated:** March 2026  
**App:** Healthcare Planner (Android / iOS)

This policy explains what information Healthcare Planner collects, how we use it, who we share it with, and how we keep it secure. By using the app, you agree to this policy.

---

## 1. What the app does

Healthcare Planner helps you plan and track your **health and wellness**. You can:

- **Set up your profile:** name, age, biological sex, weight, height, activity level, and goals (e.g. lose fat, gain muscle, eat better, mental balance).
- **Sign in with Google** (optional): to sync your data across devices and use your email and profile photo.
- **Create a weekly plan:** meals and workouts you add yourself or get from the in-app coach.
- **Track nutrition:** log meals, save recipes (with optional photos), and see calories and macros.
- **Manage exercise routines:** add exercises, set reminders, and track your progress.
- **Use the Coach AI:** get personalized tips on nutrition, exercise, and wellness via text (and optionally voice or photos). Available with a premium subscription.
- **Receive reminders:** local notifications for your workouts and other alerts you set.
- **See ads:** the free version may show ads; the premium version is ad-free.

The app is **not intended for users under 13**. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

---

## 2. What information we collect and why

### Information you provide

- **Profile data** (name, age, sex, weight, height, activity level, goals): used to personalize your experience, calculate calorie goals, and tailor the Coach AI’s advice.
- **Email and profile photo:** only if you sign in with Google; used to identify your account and show your profile in the app.
- **Recipes and meal photos:** stored so you can view and manage them; if you’re signed in, they may be synced to your account.
- **Messages to the Coach AI** (text, and optionally voice or images): used only to provide you with personalized responses and to improve the service.

### Information from how you use the app

- **Your plans, notes, reminders, and daily logs:** stored on your device and, if you’re signed in, synced to your account so you can access them from other devices.
- **Coach AI conversations:** kept so you can continue the conversation and so the coach can give you consistent advice.
- **Usage information:** we may keep basic usage data (e.g. how much you use the Coach AI) to apply subscription limits and improve the app. This is linked to your account when you are signed in.

We **do not sell** your personal information to third parties.

---

## 3. Where your data is stored

- **On your device:** your preferences, weekly plan, notes, reminders, recipes, daily logs, and a local copy of your Coach AI conversations are stored on your phone or tablet.
- **In the cloud:** if you sign in with Google, we sync the data above to secure cloud storage so you can use it on other devices. This is done using trusted infrastructure and industry-standard security practices.

---

## 4. Sharing with third parties

We work with service providers that help us run the app (e.g. sign-in, cloud storage, the Coach AI, and ads in the free version). They only receive what is necessary to provide those features and must protect your data according to their own policies and our agreements.

We do **not** share your data with third parties for their marketing, and we do **not** sell your data.

---

## 5. App permissions

The app only asks for permissions it needs to work:

- **Internet:** to sync your data, run the Coach AI, show ads (free version), and handle sign-in.
- **Notifications:** to send you the reminders you set (e.g. for workouts).
- **Precise alarms** (where applicable): to trigger those reminders at the right time.
- **After device restart:** to reschedule your reminders.
- **Vibration:** so you can feel when a reminder arrives.

If you use certain features, we may also ask for:

- **Camera or photo library:** for profile pictures and photos of meals or recipes (including when you send a photo to the Coach AI).
- **Microphone:** only if you send voice messages to the Coach AI.

You can turn these permissions off in your device settings; some features may then stop working.

---

## 6. Security

We care about keeping your data safe:

- **Secure connections:** data sent between the app and our services is encrypted.
- **Sign-in:** we use Google Sign-In; we do not store your Google password.
- **Abuse prevention:** we use measures to reduce unauthorized or abusive use of our services.
- **Your device:** data stored on your device is kept in the app’s private storage.

We recommend keeping your device locked and not sharing your Google account with others.

---

## 7. Your rights

You can:

- **View and update** your profile in the app (e.g. in settings or edit profile).
- **Stop using the Coach AI** and, where the app allows, delete or manage past conversations.
- **Sign out** of your account; data already synced will remain until you ask us to delete it.
- **Revoke permissions** (notifications, camera, microphone) in your device settings.
- **Ask us to delete your account and data** by contacting us (see Contact below). Uninstalling the app will remove data stored only on that device.

If you are in a region with laws like the GDPR, you may also have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict your data, or to complain to a data protection authority.

---

## 8. How long we keep data

- **On your device:** until you delete the app or clear its data.
- **In the cloud:** for as long as your account exists and you do not ask for deletion. We may keep some usage-related data for a limited time where needed for billing or legal reasons.
